How It Works

Our platform makes finding and claiming lost items straightforward

1. Report Your Item

Whether you've lost something valuable or found an item, our intuitive reporting system makes it easy to submit detailed information. Include photos, location details, and distinctive characteristics to improve matching accuracy.

Our system categorizes and indexes each submission to maximize the chances of successful recovery.

Report an Item

2. Matching & Discovery

Our powerful matching algorithm analyzes item descriptions, images, locations, and timestamps to identify potential matches between lost and found items.

You can also manually search our database using filters to find items that match your description, improving your chances of recovery.

Browse Items

3. Verification & Return

When you find your item, submit a claim with proof of ownership. Our quality checkers carefully review each claim to ensure legitimacy while protecting privacy and security.

Once verified, we facilitate the safe return of your belongings, completing the recovery process with confidence.

About Our Platform

Lost & Found was born from a simple yet powerful idea: to create a more efficient way to reunite people with their lost belongings.

Our platform combines innovative technology with human verification to create a secure, reliable lost and found system that works for everyone. We use a semi-automated matching process where our application intelligently identifies potential matches between lost and found items, while our dedicated quality checkers provide the human touch needed to ensure accuracy and trustworthiness.

What sets our platform apart is our commitment to:

  • Accuracy - Our matching algorithm considers multiple factors including item descriptions, locations, timing, and visual similarity
  • Security - We protect user privacy and implement verification systems to prevent fraudulent claims
  • Simplicity - Our intuitive interface makes it easy to report, search for, and claim items
  • Transparency - Users can track the status of their reports and claims throughout the process
